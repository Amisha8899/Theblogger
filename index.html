Lifestyle

Love the life you live. January 20, 2023

Wikipedia defines a lifestyle as the way a person lives. This includes patterns of social relations, consumption, entertainment, and dress. A lifestyle typically also reflects an individual's attitudes, values or worldview. A glamorous lifestyle means you indulge in upscale, high-profile pursuits and live luxuriously.

Craig Machey

Fashion

Fashion You can buy but style you possess. March 23, 2023

For fashion, visuals are key. It’s no wonder, that many lovers of style turn to Instagram and fashion blogs to get their fill of all things fashion. Instagram is great when it comes to OOTDs—outfit of the day—and quick style inspirations, but blogs are where the serious fashionistas go to write about trends, advice, and the latest fashion innovations.

Laurel Dency

Travel

I haven't been everywhere but it's on my list. June 20, 2023

Every time we had to plan a trip, we buy a guide about the country they were going to visit. But today, thanks to Internet, everything is easier, and of course, planning a trip. Today you don’t have to buy a different guide every time you want to travel, you just type on your computer what you are looking for and you get your answers immediately.

Atika Bana
